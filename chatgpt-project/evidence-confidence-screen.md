# Evidence Confidence Screen

## Confidence ratings

| Rating | Criteria |
|---|---|
| High | Clear baseline, approved target, current actual, stable source, named measure owner, recent evidence, no major contradiction. |
| Medium | Some evidence exists, but one or two items are partial, provisional, proxy-based, or still maturing. |
| Low | Missing baseline, unclear target, no actual, weak source, missing owner, stale evidence, or unsupported claim. |

## Evidence gap categories

- Missing baseline.
- Missing target.
- Missing actual.
- Unclear measure owner.
- Weak or self-reported source.
- Proxy metric only.
- Stale measurement.
- Financial validation missing.
- Benefit not tied to original approved claim.

## Output language

Use direct wording:

- "Confidence is low because no baseline or actual has been provided."
- "Confidence is medium because adoption is evidenced, but outcome impact is still a proxy."
- "Confidence is high enough for review, pending human acceptance of the source and method."
