# Realization Risk Screen

## Risk categories

| Risk | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Measurement risk | The benefit may exist but cannot be reliably measured. |
| Ownership risk | No accountable measure owner or sponsor is clear. |
| Timing risk | Benefit window has not arrived or has slipped. |
| Dependency risk | Another initiative, team, system, vendor, or process blocks realization. |
| Adoption risk | Users are not using the new process, tool, or behavior enough to create value. |
| Sustainability risk | Early benefit may not hold after launch, pilot, or executive attention fades. |
| Financial validation risk | Claimed financial impact lacks finance-approved method or confirmation. |
| Strategic relevance risk | The original value claim may no longer matter because context changed. |

## Risk rating

- **High**: threatens credibility of the benefit or requires executive / owner action.
- **Medium**: manageable but should stay visible.
- **Low**: monitored, with no material concern at this time.

## Rule

Do not treat delivery completion as value realization. A delivered initiative can still have high realization risk.
